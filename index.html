Introduction to the game

Dating back to the 15th centruy, chess is a board game where you play against an opponent and the goal of the game is to capture or "checkmate" the king. Use your wit, tactics and strategy to win this wonderful game By using the pawns, your bishops, rooks, knights and your queen, you protect your king from danger while trying to capture your opponents king. A wonderful war that can help you improve your cognitive thinking as well.

So what are you waiting for? Let's learn the game!
